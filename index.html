The Anthology

Three Realms of Dread and Wonder

Lucid Terrors is a collection of original short stories spanning the outermost edges of speculative fiction — where the known universe ends and something older begins.

🐙

Cosmic Horror

The universe is indifferent — and it is watching. Stories of incomprehensible entities, forbidden knowledge, and the fragile sanity of those who look too long into the void.

🛸

Science Fiction

Futures that feel uncomfortably close. Artificial minds, dying worlds, first contacts gone wrong, and the quiet horror of technologies we no longer understand.

⚗️

Fantasy

Ancient magic twisted by modern desperation. Portals to realms that want to be entered. Bargains with things that call themselves gods, and sometimes are.
